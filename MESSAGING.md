# RelayKit Messaging

## Category

`Harness Augmentation`

## Core Narrative

RelayKit augments existing harnesses like Codex, Claude Code, Gemini CLI, and Antigravity so one operator can run parallel lanes, assign deliberate roles, and keep the human in charge of coordination.

## Lead Line

`Harness augmentation for multi-tool, human-in-the-loop parallel execution.`

## Plain-English Explanation

RelayKit makes existing AI harnesses behave like coordinated lanes in one operator-directed system instead of a pile of disconnected assistants.

## Mechanisms

These support the story. They are not the story.

- task intake
- lane recommendation
- harness onboarding
- prompt-stack resolution
- checkpoints
- handoffs
- learning
- personas

## Product Rule

Lead with harness augmentation and operator-controlled parallel work.

Do not lead with personalization alone, task flow alone, or routing alone.
